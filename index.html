Climate Change and Borders

Explore the effects of climate change on shrinking glaciers and the consequent shifts of the watershed that defines the national borders of Italy, Austria, Switzerland and France.

Old Borders

Due to global warming and shrinking Alpine glaciers, the watershed has shifted consistently. A new concept of movable border has thus been introduced by the Italian Government into national legislation.

In 2016, the Italian Limes team installed a network of monitoring units on the surface of the glacier. The data collected allows the team to monitor the movements of the ice sheet, the watershed and the new border.
